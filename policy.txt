    Railway Cancellation and Refund Policy
    
    Cancellation Timeline and Refunds:
    1. Cancellations made 24 hours or more before departure: 75% refund of ticket price
    2. Cancellations made between 12-24 hours before departure: 50% refund of ticket price
    3. Cancellations made less than 12 hours before departure: 25% refund of ticket price
    4. No refund will be provided for no-shows (passengers who don't board the train)
    
    Refund Processing Details:
    - All refunds are processed within 7-10 business days from the date of cancellation
    - Refund will be credited to the original payment method used for booking
    - A processing fee of ₹50 applies to all cancellations
    - For cash bookings, refund will be issued as a railway credit note
    
    Special Circumstances for Full Refund:
    - Medical emergencies: Full refund available with a valid doctor's certificate submitted within 48 hours
    - Train cancellations by railway: Full refund is automatically processed within 3-5 business days
    - Natural disasters or government restrictions: Case-by-case review, typically full refund granted
    - Death in family: Full refund with death certificate
    
    Ticket Modification Policy:
    - Date changes allowed up to 24 hours before departure with ₹100 modification fee
    - Name changes not permitted once booking is confirmed
    - Class upgrades available subject to availability, price difference applies
    - Downgrades result in refund of difference minus ₹150 processing fee
    
    Group Booking Cancellations:
    - Groups of 4+ passengers: Cancellation charges apply per person
    - 25% group discount may be forfeited if partial cancellation made
    - Full group cancellations follow standard refund policy
